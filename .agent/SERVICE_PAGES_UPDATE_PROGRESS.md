### Key Components
1. **Hero Section**: Breadcrumb navigation, large heading, description, CTA buttons, stats grid
2. **Services Grid**: 2x2 or 3x2 grid with icons, titles, descriptions, and "Learn More" links
3. **Use Cases**: 4-column grid showcasing real-world applications
4. **Benefits**: Two-column layout with checklist and highlight cards
5. **CTA Section**: Gradient background with centered content and action buttons
